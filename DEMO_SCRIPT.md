# StackCred Demo Video Script 🎥

**Target Duration**: 2-3 Minutes
**Goal**: Show the end-to-end flow of verifying a GitHub profile and minting a Credential NFT on Stacks.

---

## Scene 1: Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)
*   **Visual**: Show the **StackCred Landing Page** (Home).
*   **Action**: Scroll down slowly to show the "Features" and "Leaderboard".
*   **Voiceover**: 
    > "Welcome to StackCred, a decentralized credentialing platform built for the Stacks Talent App Challenge. StackCred lets developers verify their open-source contributions and mint them as permanent, on-chain NFTs."

## Scene 2: Wallet Connection (0:30 - 0:50)
*   **Visual**: Click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top right.
*   **Action**: Select Xverse/Hiro wallet and approve the connection.
*   **Voiceover**: 
    > "First, we connect our Stacks wallet using the Stacks Connect integration. It supports both Mainnet and Testnet."

## Scene 3: Scoring & Verification (0:50 - 1:30)
*   **Visual**: Scroll to the **Check Eligibility** card.
*   **Action**: 
    1. Enter a valid GitHub username (e.g., `muneeb` or your own).
    2. Click **Check**.
    3. Show the loading state and then the Result Card.
*   **Voiceover**: 
    > "Here's the magic. We verify your GitHub activity in real-time. I'll enter a username... and boom. StackCred calculates a 'Builder Score' based on repositories and commit history. If you qualify, you unlock the ability to mint."

## Scene 4: Minting the NFT (1:30 - 2:00)
*   **Visual**: The **Mint Credential** button is now visible (green).
*   **Action**: 
    1. Click **Mint Credential**.
    2. Show the Stacks Wallet popup appearing.
    3. Scroll through the transaction details (Function: `mint`).
    4. Click **Confirm**.
*   **Voiceover**: 
    > "Qualifying builders can mint their 'StackCred' NFT directly to their wallet. This transaction interacts with our Clarity smart contract, adhering to the SIP-009 standard."

## Scene 5: Leaderboard & Closing (2:00 - 2:30)
*   **Visual**: Scroll down to the **Leaderboard**.
*   **Action**: Hover over a few top users.
*   **Voiceover**: 
    > "Once minted, you join the global leaderboard of verified builders. StackCred is verifying the next generation of talent on Bitcoin. Thanks for watching!"

---

**Tips for Recording:**
*   Clean your desktop/browser tabs.
*   Ensure your wallet is on **Testnet** (if using devnet/testnet).
*   Zoom in slightly (110%) for better readability.
